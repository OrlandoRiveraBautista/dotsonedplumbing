Drain Repairs

It’s pretty tempting to ignore a slow-moving drain and continue your daily routine. After all, it’s just a minor inconvenience, right? It may seem so initially, but a clogged drain can cause bigger—more expensive—consequences. The cause of your slow drain could be a clog, or grease buildup that’s narrowing your pipes, or a few other common problems. Dotson Ed plumbing can help you find the root of your obstruction and get it repaired before you end up with leaking—or worse, broken—pipes and a water-damaged living space.
